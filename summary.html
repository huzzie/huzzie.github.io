Conclusion

The dataset was imbalanced and SMOTE method was used for balanding the data. Surprisingly, there was no significant relationship between variables to distinguish the patterns between attrited customers and churned customers. KNN, Random Forest, Decision Tree were used to predict the churned customers, and it seemed Random Forest model was the best model, which scored ROC-AUC value 0.98. With these model, we could find that total transation count and total transaction amount variables are the significant variable in the model. Therefore, finance industry needs to make plans or deals for transation amount/count to prevent customer churns.
